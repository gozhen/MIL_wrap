models/: 
	stores the trained models by our algorithm.
	

std/: 
	the standard input sequence files for our MIL algorithm.

	format:
	[sequence] [label]
	e.g.:
	"
	TTACCCAAAAGCCGGGTCGCACGTGGCCTCAACTA p
	CTGGGAGCACGTGGTTTCAAATCGTTCGTTAGATT p
	GCAACTCACGCGTGACATAAAGCGAGACGCCCAGG p
	CGATCGCCGTATAAAGACCTCTGAACACGTCATTT p
	GCTTCAACTATTATATCACGCGGATCACGTGTTTC p
	TCCACACGGACCACGTGGTTCAAAATGCTTCTGCC p
	CATTGGTGTGGGACTCTCACAAACACGTGAGGTAA p
	"

peak_from_encode_in_fa/:
	Peak file examples from ENCODE in fasta format.
